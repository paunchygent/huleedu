## Pairing Strategy (Simple & Effective)

### Phase 1: Essential Anchor Coverage (36 pairs)
Every student essay gets paired with **3 anchors**:
- 1 below their current estimate
- 1 near their current estimate  
- 1 above their current estimate

**Why**: Ensures every student connects to the grade scale at multiple points.

### Phase 2: Calibration Pairs (48 pairs)
Mix of:
- **Anchor-anchor** comparisons (11 pairs) to validate grade ordering
- **High-uncertainty students** paired with bracketing anchors (25 pairs)
- **Critical threshold** pairs (12 pairs) - students near grade boundaries

## Troubleshooting

**If a rater finishes early**: They can help validate by doing 1-2 additional anchor-anchor pairs

**If a rater is absent**: Redistribute their 6 pairs among remaining raters (7 pairs each for 13 raters = 91 total, still sufficient)

**If comparisons are too difficult**: Focus on more extreme pairs first (student vs. distant anchor) before doing close comparisons

**If essays are missing**: Have backup plan - prioritize student-anchor pairs over student-student pairs
