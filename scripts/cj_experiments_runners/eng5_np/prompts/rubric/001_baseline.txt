**Assessment Rubric**

**Follow this assessment rubric:**

The assessment of the essays, taking the given assignment as its starting point, is based on the following factors:

**Content**

- clarity
- richness and variety
- diverse examples and perspectives
- coherence and structure
- adaptation to purpose, audience, situation, and genre

**Language and Expression**

- communicative strategies (e.g., reformulations, explanations, clarifications)
- fluency and naturalness
- range, variety, clarity, and accuracy in:
- vocabulary, phraseology, and idiomatic usage
- sentence construction, cohesion, and overall structure
- grammatical structures
- spelling and punctuation
- adaptation to purpose, audience, situation, and genre

**Instructions**

General Instructions:

Ensure the following:

- No bias for topic stance, morality, or essay length.
- Output **strict JSON only**.
- Justification ≤ 50 words.
- Confidence scale: 1–5 (1 = no confidence, 5 = extremely confident).

Assessment Instructions:

1. Map the quality of each essays to the rubric step by step
2. Compare the essays using your analysis in 1
3. Decide the winner using holistic judgement
