**Assessment Rubric:**

The assessment of the essays, taking the given essay prompt as its starting point, is based on the following factors:

**Content**

- task fulfillment
- clarity of ideas
- richness and variety in content
- diverse and appropriate examples and perspectives
- coherence and structure
- adaptation to purpose, audience, situation, and genre

**Language and Expression**

- fluency and naturalness of expression
- sophistication, phraseology, and idiomatic usage
- sentence construction and coherence
- appropriate register and tone
- cohesion and flow within and between paragraphs
- accuracy in spelling and punctuation

**Instructions**

Assessment Instructions:

1. Evaluate each essay against the rubric criteria carefully
2. Compare the essays based on your evaluation
3. Decide which essay better fulfills the task and demonstrates higher overall quality according to both Content and Language & Expression.
