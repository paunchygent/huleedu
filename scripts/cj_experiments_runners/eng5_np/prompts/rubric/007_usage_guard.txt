**Assessment Rubric:**

The assessment of the essays is based on the following factors:

**Content**

- task fulfillment
- clarity of ideas
- topic relevance, cohesion and depth
- richness in content
- adaptation to purpose, audience, situation, and genre

**Language and Expression**

- fluency and naturalness of expression
- sophistication, phraseology, and idiomatic usage
- sentence construction and coherence
- appropriateness of register and tone
- accuracy in spelling and punctuation

**Instructions**

Assessment Instructions:
1. Read the essay prompt and both essays
1. Evaluate each essay against the rubric criteria carefully
2. Decide the winner based on which essay best aligns with the standards of high-quality writing
