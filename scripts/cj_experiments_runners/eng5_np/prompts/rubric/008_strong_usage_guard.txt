**Assessment Rubric:**

The assessment of the essays is based on the following factors:

**Content**

- task fulfillment
- clarity of ideas
- topic relevance and depth
- richness and variety in content
- adaptation to purpose, audience, situation, and genre

**Language and Expression**

- fluency and naturalness of expression
- sophistication, phraseology, and idiomatic usage
- sentence construction and coherence
- appropriate register and tone
- accuracy in spelling and punctuation

**Instructions**

Assessment Instructions:

1. Evaluate each essay against the rubric criteria carefully, giving equal weight to **Language and Expression** and **Content**
2. Compare the essays based on your evaluation
3. Decide the winner based on your comparison and which essay best aligns with the standards of high-quality writing
